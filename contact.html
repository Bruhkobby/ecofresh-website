Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products really non-toxic?

Yes! All EcoFresh products are made with plant-based ingredients and contain no harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, or toxic substances. They're safe for your family and pets.

How do I place an order?

Currently, our products are available through select retail partners. Visit our website for a list of stores near you, or contact us for online ordering information.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship within the United States and Canada. We're working on expanding to more countries soon. Contact us for updates on international availability.

What makes EcoFresh different?

We combine powerful cleaning performance with genuine environmental responsibility. Our products work as well as traditional cleaners but without compromising your health or the planet.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! We never test on animals and all our products are certified cruelty-free. We're committed to ethical practices at every step.

Can I recycle the packaging?

Yes! All our packaging is made from recycled materials and is 100% recyclable. We encourage you to recycle our bottles and reduce waste.
